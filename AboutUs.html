HOW DECORIST WORKS

Our Online Interior Design Process How does DECORIST work? We’re glad you asked. We’re the only online interior design service that combines the expertise and personalization you get from working with a real designer with the power of virtual room design. The result? You can see interior design ideas in your exact room, collaborate online with your designer, and shop your entire look (including all furniture and decor) in one easy checkout.

What you should already know

Background information before getting into the topic:

1. Show Us Your Room

  • Getting started is easy! Use our iOS app to quickly scan your room or upload photos to our site. Don’t worry about clearing out the clutter, we’ll take care of that virtually. Then we’ll get to work and turn your photos into an exact, to-scale 3D version of your room.

    • 2. Help Us Get to Know You

  • Interior design is personal so we want to get to know you, your space, and anyone else (kiddos, pets, partners) who lives there. Take our style quiz and tell us more details about your room, budget, and lifestyle. Once we have all your info, our designers will get to work creating designs you’ll love.

    • 3. Get Your First Design

  • Let the fun begin! Our designer will send you an interactive, 3D version of your exact room. You’ll see an immersive view of layout and design ideas for your space
